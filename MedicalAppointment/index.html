What It is?

MediApp

Medical appointment (MediApp) is the new system in which medical appointments never have been easier. In MediApp you can (as a Pacient) create an appointment to go with a doctor, as a Doctor you can manage your appointments and control the pacients dates, also as a company you can save a lot of money, because you will dont need more paper for dates for pacients. This whole system works by making an appointment via MediApp webpage or through our mobile MediApp at anytime and anywhere.

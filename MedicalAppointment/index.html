What It is?

MediApp

Medical appointment (MediApp) is a new system we propose in which doing an appointment to go to the doctor is more easy for the patient and for the doctor. This whole system works by making an appointment via this webpage. You will be able to look at the time the doctor is available, and in that precise moment you can book that time , or you can also cancel letting available that time for another patient.
